About my shop

Ryness Lighting and Electrical was founded as a single store in Goodge St, London in 1961. Over the years the business expanded from a single specialist shop into a general electrical supplies company. Today Ryness is an electrical supplies and lighting expert with an unparalleled network of 14 shops in central London, providing a complete range of electrical supplies and lighting solutions for the home and trade.
